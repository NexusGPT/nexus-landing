# Nexus - Business-led Enterprise AI Platform

## About

Nexus is a business-led enterprise AI platform that enables business teams to transform their workflows into autonomous AI agents in days, not months. The platform is designed for non-technical users while maintaining enterprise-grade security standards.

## Platform Overview

Nexus is the only enterprise platform where business teams transform their workflows into autonomous agents in days, not months. The platform provides:

- **No-code AI agent creation**: Business teams can build and deploy AI agents without coding skills
- **Enterprise-grade security**: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and ISO 42001 certified
- **Rapid deployment**: Deploy production-ready agents in 3-5 days instead of 6-12 months
- **Technology agnostic**: Connect any system, choose any AI model, integrate any tool with 3,000+ plugins plus custom API support
- **Intelligent automation**: Agents make decisions, adapt to situations, and bring humans into the loop when needed
- **Universal deployment**: Deploy agents anywhere - Office apps, Teams, Slack, email, phone
